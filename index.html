AI-Powered Solution for Smarter Farming

GreenSence AI helps you detect plant issues early, optimize resources, and automate your farm — all from a single platform.

GreenSence AI is an intelligent farming assistant that empowers agricultural professionals with real-time diagnostics, data-driven decisions, and automated farm operations. By combining satellite imagery, AI diagnostics, and sensor data, we help you identify plant diseases, spot nutrient imbalances, and take action faster — for healthier crops and higher yields.

GreenSence AI Features

  • AI-Powered Diagnosis Instantly analyze plant photos to detect diseases, pests, and deficiencies with high accuracy.
  • Satellite & Sensor Integration Get a full-field overview using real-time satellite data, soil sensors, and weather tracking.
  • Automated Farm Control Trigger smart responses like irrigation, fertilization, or pesticide spraying — remotely and automatically.
  • Smart Alerts & Insights Receive early warnings about risks like drought, frost, or infestations, personalized to your crops and region.

Product benefits

GreenSence AI is an innovative platform combining artificial intelligence, satellite analytics, IoT sensors, and greenhouse automation to revolutionize agriculture. Our platform enables farmers and gardeners to:

  • Accurately detect plant diseases and nutritional deficiencies.
  • Optimize irrigation, fertilization, and greenhouse lighting conditions.
  • Effectively manage crops using integrated satellite imagery and sensor data.
  • Reduce operational costs, environmental impacts, and improve plant productivity.

Our Vision

Our vision is a future where advanced agricultural technology is accessible to every farm, greenhouse, and garden — enhancing productivity, sustainability, and profitability. Our mission is to empower growers to cultivate healthier plants more efficiently while reducing environmental impact and resource consumption.
