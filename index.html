Product benefits

PlantVision AI is an innovative platform combining artificial intelligence, satellite analytics, IoT sensors, and greenhouse automation to revolutionize agriculture. Our platform enables farmers and gardeners to:

  • Accurately detect plant diseases and nutritional deficiencies.
  • Optimize irrigation, fertilization, and greenhouse lighting conditions.
  • Effectively manage crops using integrated satellite imagery and sensor data
  • Reduce operational costs, environmental impacts, and improve plant productivity

Our Vision

Our vision is a future where advanced agricultural technology is accessible to every farm, greenhouse, and garden — enhancing productivity, sustainability, and profitability. Our mission is to empower growers to cultivate healthier plants more efficiently while reducing environmental impact and resource consumption.

Project Roadmap:

  • Phase 1 (Q2 2025):

    • Launch of PlantVision AI MVP
    • Testing core AI-based plant diagnosis capabilities

  • Phase 2 (Q3-Q4 2025):

    • Integration of IoT sensors and real-time satellite analytics
    • Inclusion of weather station data and greenhouse automation features

  • Phase 3 (Q1-Q2 2026):

    • Implementation of remote machinery management (tractors, drones, automated irrigation)
    • Launch of mobile app and full web-based dashboard

  • Phase 4 (Q3 2026 onwards):

    • Expansion to international markets (Europe, USA, Middle East)
    • Strategic partnerships with agricultural laboratories, greenhouse manufacturers, and IoT technology providers
